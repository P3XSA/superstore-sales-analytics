# Superstore Sales Performance Analysis
*By Pesandu Randisa*

---

## Executive Summary
This dashboard represents 4 years of sales data of superstore to indentify and study the patterns in sales and identify the opportunities to improve in each area such as, regions, category, discounts, customer segments, and more...

---

## Key Findings
- Total Sales: 2.30M | Total Profit: 286.40K | Total Orders: 5009 | Profit Margin: 12.47%
- Sales peak during Q4 (November-December Holdiay Season)
- Top Category: Technology.
- Top Region: West (Highest profits).
- Top Product: Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier.
- Top Segment: Consumer (Around 50% of total sales).
- States like California, New York, Texas & Washington have higher sales.
- Discounts higher than 20% have significant negative impact on profits.
- Standard class shipping dominates in sales by ship mode (More than $1.3M in sales).
- Highest performing Sub-Category in each Category:
    Technology: Phones
    Furniture: Chairs
    Office Supplies: Storage

---

## Recommendations
1. **Be prepared with more stocks and marketing for Q4 each year since sales peaks during November-December holiday season.
2. **Focus marketing on Technology category.
3. **South & Central regions need targeted campaigns to boost sales.
4. **Keep loyal customers with rewards.
5. **Attract new customers with offers that won't hurt the profitablility in the long run.
6. **Try to keep all the discounts under 20%.
7. **Can offer special whole sale deals for Corporate and Home Office segments to boost sales.
